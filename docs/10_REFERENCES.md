# References
Space Radiator — Venus Cooling Project

---

## Purpose

This document lists **scientific and educational references** relevant to the
Space Radiator analysis. Sources focus on radiative heat transfer, planetary
energy balance, Venus climate, and large-scale thermal systems.

All references are foundational and widely accepted.

---
